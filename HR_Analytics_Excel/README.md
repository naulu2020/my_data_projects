# HR Analytics Dashboard (Excel)
## Objectives
- Analysing employee data to identify trends in retention, performance, reward, performance and department distribution
## Data used
- Sample HR dataset that contains employee demographic, tenure, salary, department, performance rating and job type.
## Tools
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Formulas, and Charts)
## Main Insights
- The distribution of employees across the organisation is unevenly as Female consitutes 62% as opposed to Male who accounts for 37%.
- Female employees ($64651) have a higher average salary than male employees ($62632), with females earning approximately 2,019 more on average.
- While 72% of employees are on permanent contracts, the remaining 28% are in contracting roles.
- The average year of service within the organisation is nearly 10 years.
- The salary distribution is uneven across departments where Finance and IT recorded the highest salary expenses with  19.8% and 17.07% respectively.
- There are 7 employees who spent 20 years of service which was the highest in the company. 
## Dashboard
The Excel dashboard allows interactive exploration of HR metrics.
